Account Deletion Request

DietWise respects your privacy and provides users with the ability to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your DietWise account and associated data, please contact our support team at:

zeroid.tech.in@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your DietWise account in your request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Profile details
  • Nutrition and diet-related records
  • App preferences and settings
  • Other personal data associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained where required by applicable laws, regulations, security requirements, fraud prevention, or legitimate business purposes.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days after verification.
